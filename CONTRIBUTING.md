# Contributing to Open-Pact License

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the Open-Pact License! 

## How to Contribute

### Review the Text
The most valuable contribution during Soft Launch is reading [LICENSE.md](LICENSE.md) and [NOTES.md](NOTES.md) and identifying:
* Ambiguous language
* Unintended edge cases
* Missing legal protections
* Inconsistencies between sections

### Submit Feedback
* Open an [Issue](https://github.com/open-pact/license/issues) for questions, concerns, or findings.
* Use the [Soft Launch Feedback](https://github.com/open-pact/license/issues/1) thread for general feedback.
* Submit a Pull Request for specific text changes with a clear explanation of the problem and proposed solution.

### Style Guide
* Keep legal text formal and precise.
* Keep NOTES.md accessible to non-lawyers.
* When proposing changes, reference the specific section and principle affected.
