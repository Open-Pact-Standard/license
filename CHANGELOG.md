## [1.1] - 2026-04-05 (Soft Launch)

### Structural
- Introduced **License Steward** pattern (solo dev OR Guild governance)
- Added **Functionally Equivalent Work** definition (anti-clean-room bypass)
- Added **Total Workforce** metric (employees + contractors + autonomous AI)
- Fees moved from legal text to **Project Registry** (stablecoin/fiat agnostic)

### Enforcement
- **Canary Token Enforcement** with semantic integration (non-rebuttable evidence)
- **Binding Arbitration** via New York Convention (170+ jurisdictions)
- Disgorgement damages (3 options: actual+disgorgement, statutory, or 10x fee)
- Whistleblower anonymity + anti-retaliation provisions

### Dependencies (Section 18)
- Clear separation of licenses (no viral effect on dependencies)
- Shadow Dependency Prohibition (anti-evasion)
- Anti-Poisoning clause for incompatible license combinations
- Good Faith Declaration with 30-day cure window

### Governance
- Steward Removal for Malfeasance (20% petition, 5 grounds, 90-day cure)
- Guild Membership petition right (3+ contributions)
- Human-only voting for Legal Entities
- Copyright grant survives Steward disappearance

### Adversarial Testing
- 7 rounds of automated stress testing
- 48 findings identified and patched
- Complete audit trail in FINAL_REPORT.md
